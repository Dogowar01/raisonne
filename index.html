Everything the register can do
The three registers
EditionsThe print-editions ledger — series, sales, collectors, market, palette studio, all of it.
AppsA shelf of what you're building — status, stack, links, notes. Group by status, grid or list.
WritingA lightweight index of pages — type, status, a word-count meter, synopsis.
The rooms
ExhibitionClick any artwork to view it large — ← → walks the gallery, Esc leaves. Works in all three registers.
The cardClick a plate's caption (or a row) to edit it — full details live here.
MarketEditions only — stall mode: tap a work to sell its next number; day total and undo up top.
CollectorsEditions only — your buyers, contacts, purchase history, waitlists.
StudioAnalytics for the active register — sales & margins on Editions, stack & momentum on Apps, the word ledger on Writing.
PaletteColour tools: eyedropper, harmonies, duotone, notan, wall mockup, exports to Procreate/Adobe/GIMP.
Marks & notesIn Palette, switch to Draw or Note to sketch on a print or pin notes to it. They live only there — a small brass dot on the plate shows a work carries them (click the dot to jump in).
CatalogueEditions only — a public collector catalogue, no sold counts, no private notes.
VaultStorage health, backup, and snapshots — automatic rollback points; one-click Restore brings back deleted or changed records without touching newer ones.
ImportOn Editions, drag print images anywhere onto the window — titles are read from filenames.
